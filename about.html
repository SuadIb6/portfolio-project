About me

Aspiring front-end developer from London,England

I'm Suad, a primary school teacher who is now beginning a new journey into coding. I have been teaching for 11 years, teaching children key skills that will support them in their learning journey and the wider world. It has been a rewarding and fufilling experience seeing students discover their passion, experience success in learning, or come to understand the importance of perseverance.

Teaching has allowed me to develop my creativity and discover my own passions. This is where coding comes into place. I have always been interested in technology and how it works. In my teaching career incorporating technology has been an important part especially as it has become part of our daily lives. Coding helps us to understand technology.

You don't need to be an expert to be self-sufficient, by knowing just a little can make a huge difference to your life. Learning to code can help your life in a way you can never imagine.

My new aspiration is to become a front-end developer.As a front-end developer, I have the opportunity to make a real difference in the world. I can use my skills to build websites that educate, inform and entertain. I can also use my skills to build websites that make a difference in people’s lives.
